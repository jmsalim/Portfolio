Project: Mobile Application for Stark Vehicles

I was tasked with working on a team of two to help bring to life the mobile application for Stark Scooters. I was responsible for developing and designing all the visual elements, UI and UX interfaces and worked as the Front End developer on the project.

This was a fantastic project that I got to work on. From start to finish, I was involved and worked directly in bringing the vision of the app to life. I conducted research on all competitor platforms and dare to say ours is a much improved and polished application.

Explore the App in Action

Navigate the Map

Easily view designated riding zones and restricted areas. Locate and scan available scooters nearby or request one to your desired location.

Request a Scooter

No scooter nearby? Simply request one, and we’ll send the nearest available scooter and provide an estimated arrival time.

Track your Request

Know exactly when your ride will arrive. Track your request from start to finish and receive updates when it reaches your destination.

Unlock a Scooter

When you have your scooter, scan the QR code to unlock it. If you'd like, enter your destination to get a price estimate and check the battery range for your trip. Once you're ready, hop on and start your ride!

Enjoy your Ride

Glide through the city with ease. Follow your route, track your battery, and reach your destination smoothly. Need assistance? Support is just a tap away.

Review your History

Keep track of all your rides and requests in one place. Easily view your invoices and revisit past trips or deliveries anytime.
