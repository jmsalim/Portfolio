DTO Project: Interactive Experience

This project was created for the City of Orlando, it is coded in p5.js. It is an artistic representation of shapes, colors and movement. The nuance of the movement that combines colors and shapes is able to convey emotions. It was a great exercise in designing a sensory installation that can convey emotions and materialize concepts and ideas.

Live P5.js Sketch

You can interact with the project by using the left and right arrows, in order to navigate and change the shapes of the images.
