A-frame x P5.js

This 3D realm explores the powerful 3D rendering capabilities of A-Frame, a framework built on top of P5.js. It allows for the creation of immersive 3D environments and scenes that can be viewed and navigated, including compatibility with VR systems.

Controls Legend

To navigate the 3D space embedded below, please use the following controls:

  • Movement: Use WASD keys (W=Forward, A=Left, S=Backward, D=Right).
  • Jump: Press the Spacebar.
  • Interact/Click: Use the Mouse to look around and click on virtual buttons.
