Vista Page: Complex Culture Jam Game

**Vista Page** was a final project designed to meet the **Culture Jam Game** assignment. The objective was to create a **persuasive, non-linear narrative game** that engages with politics, advertising, or culture through **subversion** and loosely fits the notion of **Culture Jamming**.

Goal and Process

The goal was to tackle a major issue I cared about by exploring different solutions—mine, a peer's, and the implications of both—all within a playable medium. The process involved extensive research into non-linear narrative, interaction design, and the works of artists like **The Yes Men** and **Adbusters**.

The game features **visuals that enhance the aesthetic/mood** and is designed to take between **5 to 10 minutes to play**.
