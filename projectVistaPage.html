Vista Page: Complex Culture Jam Game

Vista Page was designed to create a Culture Jam Game. The objective in culture games is to create a persuasive, non-linear narrative game that engages with politics, advertising, or culture through subversionand loosely fits the notion of Culture Jamming.

Goal and Process

The goal was to recriate a belieavable yet overly "corporative" dashboard in order to poke fun of corporate greed and the current state of social media. The process involved extensive research into non-linear narrative, interaction design, and the works of artists like The Yes Men and Adbusters.

The game features visuals that enhance the aesthetic/mood and is designed to take between 5 to 10 minutes to play.
