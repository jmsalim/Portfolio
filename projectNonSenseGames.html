NonSense Games: A Micro-Game Anthology

NonSense Games is a Micro-Game Anthology blending the rapid-fire interaction of WarioWare with the surreal humor of Monty Python. The experience is a digital stream of consciousness where the player is an Oblivious Volunteer obeying arbitrary, fleeting commands.

Design Pillars

  • Absurdity: Objectives prioritize humor over logic.
  • Hyper-Focus: Simple, instinctive interactions demanded immediately.
  • Ephemerality: Nothing lasts; the rapid 8-second timer creates a disposable experience.

Core Experience Loop

The experience follows a rigid, repeating cycle defined by the 8-second timer: **Receive Task $\rightarrow$ Act $\rightarrow$ Receive Feedback $\rightarrow$ Wait $\rightarrow$ Repeat**. This design promotes confusion, amusement, and a sense of joyful futility.
