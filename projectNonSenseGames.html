NonSense Games: A Micro-Game Anthology

**NonSense Games** is a **Micro-Game Anthology** blending the rapid-fire interaction of *WarioWare* with the surreal humor of *Monty Python*. The experience is a digital stream of consciousness where the player is an **Oblivious Volunteer** obeying arbitrary, fleeting commands.

Design Pillars

  • **Absurdity:** Objectives prioritize humor over logic (e.g., "Deflate the Ego Balloon").
  • **Hyper-Focus:** Simple, instinctive interactions (one click, one keypress) demanded immediately.
  • **Ephemerality:** Nothing lasts; the rapid **4-second timer** creates a disposable experience.

Core Experience Loop

The experience follows a rigid, repeating cycle defined by the 4-second timer: **Receive Task $\rightarrow$ Act $\rightarrow$ Receive Feedback $\rightarrow$ Wait $\rightarrow$ Repeat**. This design promotes confusion, amusement, and a sense of joyful futility.
